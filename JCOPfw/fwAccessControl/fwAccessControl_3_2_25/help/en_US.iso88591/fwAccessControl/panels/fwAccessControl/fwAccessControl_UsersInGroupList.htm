Panel Name fwAccessControl/fwAccessControl_UsersInGroupList.pnl
Introduction

List of group members

This panel displays the list of members of a group. The panel is presented in Figure 1.

Figure 1: List of group members
Instructions
Restrictions
  • The panel does not update its contents when the group is changed (externally, in other UI or by the Access Control Server).
  • It is not possible to view the details of the user, as it is, for instance, in the Access Right holders list panel.

Dollar Parameters
Name Description  
$group The name of the group required
