Panel Name

fwAccessControl_PrivHoldersList.pnl
Introduction

This panel is used to display the list of holders of a privilege.

Figure 1: List of Privilege Holders panel.

The list on the left side presents the list of the groups which have selected privilege, the list on the right - the effective list of users which have the privilege.

Instructions
To query the holders of certain privilege in certain domain:
Select the domain in the "Domain" combo box, then select the privilege from "Privilege" combo box.
