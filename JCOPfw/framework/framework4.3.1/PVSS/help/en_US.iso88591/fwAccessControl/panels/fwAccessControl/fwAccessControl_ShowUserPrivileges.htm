Panel Name fwAccessControl/fwAccessControl_ShowUserPrivileges.pnl
Introduction

List of user's acces rights

This panel displays the list of members of access rights possessed by a user. The panel is presented in Figure 1.

Figure 1: List of user's access rights

The first and the second column of the table specify the access right; the last column contains the names of the groups, from which the access right comes from. Note that the the same access right may be "inherited" from many groups, and at the same time a group may "provide" many access rights.


Instructions
To include system privileges in the list:
activate the Show system privileges option at the bottom of the panel.
Restrictions
  • The panel does not update its contents when users, groups or domains are changed (externally, in other UI or by the Access Control Server).
