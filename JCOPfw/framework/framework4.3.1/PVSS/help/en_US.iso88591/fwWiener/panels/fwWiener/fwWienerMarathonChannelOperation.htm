Panel Name

fwWienerMarathonChannelOperation.pnl
Introduction

This panel is used to monitor and operate the Wiener Marathon PL512 Channel framework devices.
Instructions

The operation panel is divided into several sections. Along the top is the general information like data point name, description and the position of the channel in its parent device. There is also the possibility to go to the framework "Action" panel to peform actions on alerts and an indication of the state (using the framework colour convention) of the summary alert of the device.

The left hand side of the panel gives information on the current status and readings from the hardware. The current Power state is shown along with status flags for various error conditions. Below this, readouts of voltages, current and temperature are given.

At the bottom left, there are buttons to switch the channel ON or OFF.

The right hand side of the panel gives access to change to settings of this channel. You can change the output voltage and current. You can also change the limit values on certain hardware readings. By clicking the "Supervision Behaviour.." button in the bottom left, it is possible to view and set the behaviour that will occur when a measurement limit is exceeded. Finally, at the bottom right of the panel, you can allocate a group number to this channel.
Restrictions

None
