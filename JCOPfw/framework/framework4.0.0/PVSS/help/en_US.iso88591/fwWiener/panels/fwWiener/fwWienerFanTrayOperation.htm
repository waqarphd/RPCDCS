Panel Name

fwWienerFanTrayOperation.pnl
Introduction

This panel is used to monitor and operate the Wiener Fan Tray framework devices.
Instructions

The operation panel is divided into several sections. Along the top is the general information like data point name, description and node ID of the Wiener fan tray on its its CAN bus. There is also the possibility to go to the framework "Action" panel to peform actions on alerts and an indication of the state (using the framework colour convention) of the summary alert of the device.

Below this are three sections that give an overview of the state of the fan tray, including an overview of the fans and temperatures. When the LEDs show the standard framework OK colour, then the corresponding property is in a good state.

At the bottom left of the panel are a set of push buttons to perform actions on the fan tray. Actions are available to turn the fan tray ON/OFF and to change the fan speed.

At the bottom right of the panel is a table which gives overview information of all of the fans within in the fan tray.
Restrictions

None
