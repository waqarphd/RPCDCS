Inspecting value and status of a sub-tree of subscriptions

In the DEN, one can navigate to a particular container-node and when one clicks it, a panel appears showing you the WINCC-OA device-names of the items in that subscription.

 

     

 

To view the value and status of all the items in all subscriptions below that node, click on the "Display all DPEs, DIP items, DIP tags, values, quality and units of the DPs Hierarchy below!" button and a new panel will appear showing you all those subscriptions. Three buttons are as well available.

  • "Refresh the Table" which rescanned all subscriptions
  • "Save to Plain Text" using one line per subscription item
  • "Save to CSV File" creating a comma-separated list of data
