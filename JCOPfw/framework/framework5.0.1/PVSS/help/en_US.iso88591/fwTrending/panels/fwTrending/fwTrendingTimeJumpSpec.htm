Panel Name

fwTrendingTimeJumpSpec.pnl
Introduction

This panel is used to select a time to jump to in the history of the trend..
Instructions

When the panel loads, the current settings of the time range is displayed - the current time in the middle of the time range and the current width of the time range.

You can set a new time in the past to show in the trend and you can modify the time range width as well. This is useful when you need to jump to a specific time in the past to view some specific historical data.

Press OK to confirm the new time range or press cancel to close the panel without modifying the settings.
Restrictions None
